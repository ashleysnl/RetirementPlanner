Why RRIF minimums matter

After RRSP conversion, minimum RRIF withdrawals are mandatory and typically rise with age. In some plans, this creates taxable income higher than the spending gap.

That can increase tax drag and trigger more OAS clawback in later retirement years.

What to review in your results

  • RRIF minimum at age 71 and how it changes after that
  • Difference between required gross withdrawal and net spending need
  • Whether clawback appears after RRIF minimums begin
  • Whether depletion timing changes

Frequently asked questions

When do RRIF minimum rules start?

At your RRIF conversion age in plan settings (default 71).

Can I test RRIF rules off vs on?

Yes. Use scenario compare to check the difference directly.

Can RRIF minimums increase OAS clawback?

Yes, if total taxable income is pushed above clawback thresholds.
