What is the OAS clawback?

If taxable income rises above the annual threshold, part of OAS is repaid as recovery tax.

How do RRIF minimum withdrawals work?

After RRSP conversion to RRIF, minimum withdrawals are required each year and generally increase with age.

Why do I need to withdraw more than I spend?

Because RRSP/RRIF withdrawals are taxable. You need gross income to net your spending target after tax.
