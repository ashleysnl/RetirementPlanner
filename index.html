    Planner guide

    How this retirement planner works

    This planner starts with a guided setup, then uses the same tax-aware projection engine underneath the full SimpleKit retirement tool.

    Start with the basics

    Enter your age, current savings, annual income, and retirement target to get a strong first estimate in under a minute.

    Refine when you want

    Detailed assumptions stay hidden until you open them, so beginners get clarity first and advanced users still get depth.

    Read the result in plain language

    The top of the results explains whether you are on track, what income you can expect, and what would improve the outcome.

    What assumptions matter most

    Retirement age, lifestyle target, savings rate, and inflation usually move the answer more than minor tweaks. Government benefits and taxes matter too, but they do not need to overwhelm the first run.

    How to improve your retirement outcome

    Common wins include saving more each year, retiring a little later, reducing the target lifestyle, or improving account drawdown strategy. The planner highlights these in the result itself.
