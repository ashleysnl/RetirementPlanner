# Client Summary Mode QA Checklist

1. Open Dashboard and click `Client Summary Mode`.
2. Confirm planner sections are hidden and simplified mode appears.
3. Confirm four sections render:
   - Retirement Readiness Snapshot
   - Income Timeline
   - Key Risks
   - Strategy Suggestions
4. Edit `Prepared for`, `Scenario label`, `Prepared by`, and `Date`.
5. Refresh page and confirm metadata persists locally.
6. Click a timeline phase card and confirm selected age updates in dashboard context.
7. Click `Preview impact` on a strategy and confirm `What changed?` bullets appear.
8. Click `Apply` and `Undo preview` and verify behavior remains correct.
9. Click `Print client summary` and confirm output includes metadata + 4 sections.
10. Click `Exit Client Summary` and confirm full planner dashboard returns.
11. Verify compare scenarios, share, save/load/demo/reset still work.
12. Verify mobile iOS layout stacks cards and remains readable.
